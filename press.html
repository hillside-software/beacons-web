iBeacons Buzz. Whats New. iBeacons Press Release

JANUARY 11th

New RealEstateBeacons™ app selected as finalist for prestigious Global Innovation Award at International Builders Show in Orlando, Florida.

DENVER, CO – Since Apple introduced iBeacons a number of companies have announced innovative and exciting ways to interact with their customers. These include leading retailers like Nordstrom, Macys, Harrods, Tesco, Starbucks, Target and more. Now a new app from Hillside Software is transforming Real Estate marketing by creating new business opportunities and providing a fun and exciting way for home buyers to tour a home.

iBeacons are small, matchbox sized Bluetooth transmitters designed by Apple and, more recently, by Google. Download the highly acclaimed RealEstateBeacons™ app on the Apple App store and let strategically placed Beacons inside the home display property information on your mobile device as you pass by.

“With RealEstateBeacons™ and a mobile device home buyers now have all the facts they need in the palm of their hand”, said Alex Levy, founder and president of Hillside Software. The app lets you send feedback to the property developer or listing agent and encourages you to ask as many questions as you like. By adding this home to your ‘Favorites’ list you can recall the property at any time. Furthermore you can instantly get up-to-the-minute loan information and see what it would cost you to purchase this home. “The RealEstateBeacons™ app is designed to help sell your property and build client and vendor relationships for life”, Alex added.

The app allows you to track Home Analytics and Feedback. Behind the scenes is a database that shows all visitors to the home, how long they spent there, and which features they were interested in. This information may be invaluable as you can now track visitors as they move about the property and see which rooms or features created the most interest. The data can then be exported to your contact management software.

To learn how iBeacons have the potential to become the next great marketing tool for Real Estate Agents, Lenders, Developers, Builders and Property Managers please visit www.RealEstateBeacons.com.
